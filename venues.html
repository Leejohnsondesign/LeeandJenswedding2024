The Venues

Ceremony

The wedding ceremony will begin at 2:30pm at The Guildhall. Please can you arrive in good time to take your seats. The Guildhall is located at 28 Market Street, Poole, BH15 1NF. See map to the right.

Parking

Please don't park in front of The Guildhall itself as parking is restricted to ceremonial vehicles only. However, there is a multi-story car park and pay and display car park on Hill Street (BH15 1NR) and a multi-story car park on Old Orchard (BH15 1SB).

Reception

The reception will start after the ceremony around 4pm and is only a 15 minute drive away, please make your own way over to the reception. The reception will be held at Morden Village Hall, Morden, BH20 7DL. See map to the right.

Parking

There is plenty of parking at Morden Village Hall and cars can be left overnight.
